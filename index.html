About Me

Meet Your Educator

Dedicated to transforming nursing education through evidence-based teaching and personalized learning experiences.

Sushmita Shrestha, RN

As an Australian Registered Nurse with over 5 years of combined clinical and academic experience, I'm passionate about empowering nursing students and professionals to achieve their career goals.

My teaching philosophy centers on making complex nursing concepts accessible, engaging, and clinically relevant. Through my YouTube channel and educational platform, I've helped thousands of students successfully prepare for NCLEX, OSCE, and other professional nursing examinations.

I believe in evidence-based education that bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and clinical practice, ensuring my students are well-prepared for the challenges of modern healthcare.
